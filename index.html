How to access the source code for this site! (On Chrome)

  1. right click your mouse
  2. move your cursor down to the bottom of the menu
  3. click "inspect"
  4. Congrats! You Made it!

What else can I do with developer tools?

try to do it yourself, and then hover over the list item to see if you got it right!

  • turn off the gradient background
    1. go into inspect menu
    2. click the styles tab
    3. scroll down to the body tag
    4. uncheck the background
  • Change the title of this page
    1. go to the inspect menu
    2. expand the "head" tag
    3. right click where the text between the "title" tags
    4. Change it to whatever you like!
  • See how it would look on a smartphone or tablet
    1. go to the inspect menu
    2. press ctrl+shift+m
    3. drag the area left or right to see how responsive the page is
    4. you can also change the high from the bottom, or both from the tab in the corner!
